One beautiful spring day, in a sunny garden, there was a red rose who was very proud of her beauty. However, she was disappointed that she grew next to an ugly cactus. Every day, the rose would insult the cactus on his looks, while the cactus stayed quiet.
All the other plants in the garden tried to make the rose see sense, but she was too swayed by her own good looks.
Summer came, and the water in the garden dried up. The rose quickly began to wither. Her beautiful petals dried up and lost their color. Looking at the cactus, she saw a sparrow dip its beak into the cactus to drink some water.
The rose felt ashamed to ask for water, but she finally asked the cactus if she could have some. The kind cactus immediately agreed, helping them both survive the tough summer.
